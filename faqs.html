Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about owning Irish land

What's the idea behind BuyIreland.com?

+

Millions of people around the world come from Irish descent and many of these people will never have the opportunity to visit the country of their ancestors, or to own property here. The service enables global ownership of small Irish countryside plots as unique gifts.

Is it legal?

+

Yes. Documentation includes an official certificate of ownership and a comprehensive legal deed of assurance which sets out the contractual details and legalities of your purchase. Legal documents were prepared by Beauchamps Solicitors in Dublin. Money-back guarantee offered.

Do I really own the land?

+

Yes. You are the legal owner of that property with conditions: no building permitted to preserve natural beauty, but owners may visit anytime.

Do I get freehold or leasehold ownership of the land?

+

Neither exclusively. Renewable 20 year leasehold ownership provided, renewable AT NO CHARGE after 20 years, and again indefinitely after that.

How much land is included in each US $99.99 plot?

+

Each plot is one square foot in size. Multiple plots can be arranged adjacent if requested, with discounts for purchases exceeding five plots.

What are the shipping times and delivery options?

+

Priority airmail (15-20 business days, free) or FedEx (5-7 business days). International delays currently experienced.

Can I purchase more than one plot and have the plots located side by side?

+

Yes. Specify that you would like to have the plots located side by side using the special comments box on the order form.

How do I know you aren't reselling the same land to different people?

+

BuyIreland.com is a fully legal, tax-compliant and audited business. Detailed records maintained; reselling identical plots would be illegal.

How long will I own the land?

+

You can own the land forever if you wish. Renewable leasehold every 20 years at no charge indefinitely.

Are there any annual taxes or charges due?

+

No. Once you buy your plot you will never have to pay us another cent, ever.

Where is the land located?

+

County Roscommon, central Ireland. Approximately 75 miles from Dublin City, 140 miles from Cork City, 50 miles from Galway City and 140 miles from Belfast City.

Can I resell my land?

+

Yes. We are happy for land owners to resell their land at any time and for any amount they are offered.

What happens if BuyIreland.com disappears?

+

Legal ownership vests in the owner through deed documents. Property cannot be claimed as company assets.

What size is the package I receive when I place an order?

+

Standard A4 heavy-duty envelope containing certificate, deed documentation, directions, and two 6x4 photographs.

Are payments made on BuyIreland.com secure?

+

Yes. All credit card payments are processed using the highest industry standard secure SSL connection.

Does BuyIreland.com offer investment property for sale?

+

No. Our product is unique, unusual and fun but not marketed as investment property.

Does owning land with BuyIreland.com entitle me to Irish citizenship?

+

No. Ownership of property does not entitle the owner to automatic or accelerated citizenship rights.
