What You Get

It is our aim to bring a smile to the face of everyone who opens a package from BuyIreland.com.

The personalised property ownership pack includes:

Everything included in your personalized Irish land package

Certificate of Ownership

printed on high quality parchment paper and has details of the plot owner, the individual plot number assigned to the owner, and an individual certificate number.

It features gold foil detailing and is frameable.

Deed of Assurance

The deeds fully outline our responsibilities to you as owners of the land, and also outline the rules that should be adhered to when you visit your plot.

It includes mapped paths and is printed on parchment suitable for framing.

Photographs

We include two photographs of the land with every plot. The photos are good quality prints and are also suitable for framing.

Detailed Directions

Ireland is famous for its poor signposting, so we decided it was important to include detailed directions to help you find your plot of land.

Package Details

Your order arrives in an A4-sized envelope containing:

  • One-page personalized Certificate of Ownership
  • Three-page comprehensive Deed of Assurance
  • One-page detailed directions with GPS coordinates
  • Two 6x4 inch photographs of your Irish land
