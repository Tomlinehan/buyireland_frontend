Our Story

The Story of How and Why We Bring Ireland to You

Hello and thank you for visiting. BuyIreland.com was founded by us, John Beckett and Thomas Linehan, in 2003.

John Beckett and Thomas Linehan, Founders

The Vision

Ireland holds a very special place in the hearts of millions of people around the world, yet we recognized that visiting or owning property there may be unattainable for many. We established a way for people to own a small plot of land – one square foot in size – in the beautiful rural countryside in Ireland.

Legal Foundation

Legal documents were prepared by one of the most respected law firms in Ireland to ensure everything is properly structured and legally sound.

Global Reach

Since 2003, we have served customers from over 100 countries around the world. Our work has been featured on dozens of television and radio shows around the world spanning the US, Canada, UK, South Africa, Russia, and Australia. We've been mentioned in major media outlets including The Wall Street Journal and The Washington Post.

Explore Your Land

Every purchase includes directions and detailed maps, plus Google Street View access for virtual exploration. Whether you're planning a visit or simply want to see your property from home, we make it easy to connect with your Irish land.

We're delighted to present BuyIreland.com to the world – we hope you will enjoy owning a little piece of our Emerald Isle.
